Biography

Pichai Sundararajan, known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-American business executive. He is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. Pichai joined Google in 2004, where he led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products, including Google Chrome and Chrome OS. He was instrumental in the development of Google Drive as well.

He became the CEO of Google in 2015 and later of Alphabet Inc. in 2019. Sundar is admired not just for his technical brilliance but also for his humility, leadership style, and vision. He believes in empowering people through technology and making access to information universal.
